# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People in the Philippines who need to choose a thoughtful gift for someone else, often with limited time and incomplete knowledge of what the recipient would like. They use the app primarily on a phone and want guided discovery without filling out a long questionnaire.

## Product Purpose

Help a shopper confidently choose a gift from the FlowerStore.ph catalog. The shopper selects a recipient and occasion, then swipes through ten representative gifts. The app learns from likes and dislikes, recommends the best-matching gift, explains the match, and links to the original product page. Success means the shopper reaches a credible recommendation in under two minutes and understands why it fits.

## Brand Personality

Warm, cheerful, and dependable. The experience follows the familiar FlowerStore mobile commerce rhythm: clear delivery context, real product imagery, compact controls, and a decisive shopping path.

## Anti-references

Do not resemble a dating-app clone, a childish party app, or a generic e-commerce grid. Avoid excessive hearts, confetti, gamified streaks, loud gradients, fake urgency, dense filters, and AI-chat framing. Do not invent customer ratings, delivery promises, or product claims not present in the catalog.

## Design Principles

1. Learn through choosing: every swipe should feel like useful preference feedback.
2. Show the gift first: photography and product character lead; controls remain familiar and quiet.
3. Make progress tangible: ten swipes should feel brief, clear, and purposeful.
4. Earn the recommendation: explain the result using visible signals from the shopper's choices.
5. Preserve trust: use real catalog data, transparent pricing, and direct FlowerStore.ph links.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Support keyboard and button alternatives for every swipe, visible focus states, semantic controls, sufficient contrast, responsive layouts from small phones upward, and reduced-motion preferences. Do not rely on color alone to communicate like or pass.
